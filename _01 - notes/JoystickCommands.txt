Driver Joystick 1
1. Drive Forward/Backward/Left/ Right - Large Joystick
2. Front Brakes - Side button
3. Back Brakes - Side button
4. Ramp - side bottom buttons - could be on controller Joystick - press once down, press again up
5. Side Rails - side bottom buttons - could be on controller Joystick - press once down, press again up
6. High/Low gear shifter - slider (slider forward high, slider back low)

Controller Joystick 2
1. Turret Left/Right - top bottom left/right buttons
2. Bottom Arm Up/Down - large Joystick
3. Top Arm Up/Down - small Joystick
4. Claw squeeze/release - trigger
5. Claw extendo - slidder
6. Detent for bottom arm - out and in action - one button, press once out, press again in
7. Detent for top arm - out and in action - one button, press once out, press again in
